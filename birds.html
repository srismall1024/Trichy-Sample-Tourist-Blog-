TRICHY BIRDS PARK

The city's must-see destination is Trichy Birds Park, which spans 4.02 acres and is home to hundreds of birds representing more than 40 exotic species.
The park provides an opportunity to see rare birds up close and even feed them in a secure, interactive environment, featuring everything from vibrant parrots and eye-catching macaws to playful cockatoos.
In addition to its charm, the park is home to about 40 different species of freshwater and marine fish, making it a fun place for both aquarists and birdwatchers.
Its distinctive design is based on the five ancient Tamil landscapes, each of which offers a distinct theme: Kurinji (mountains), Mullai (forest), Marutham (agriculture), Neithal (seashore), and Palai (dry land).
