Poondi Madha Basilica

Our Lady of Lourdes Basilica, popularly called Poondi Madha Basilica, is a famous Catholic pilgrimage site in Tamil Nadu, South India.
Situated in the small village of Alamelupuram-Poondi near Thirukattupalli in Thanjavur district, it was elevated to the status of Minor Basilica by Pope John Paul II on August 3, 1999.
Earlier, Cardinal Duraisamy Simon Lourdusamy had declared it a permanent pilgrimage centre dedicated to Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception.
The church's design beautifully blends Gothic and French styles, featuring statues of the Twelve Apostles, St. Francis Xavier, and Fr. Beschi. Remarkably, it also houses a relic of the True Cross.
